About

On The Wire is an acoustic duo known for blending acoustic and electric guitar with touches of piano and keyboard, creating a sound that’s both warm and dynamic. With over 20 years of shared musical experience, the duo brings a unique chemistry to the stage. Their setlist spans the last five decades, thoughtfully mixing chart-topping hits with hidden gems that deserve more love. Whether it's a nostalgic classic or an unexpected deep cut, their mission is to connect with every audience member through music that resonates.
